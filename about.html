About

Utuscribe is an independent studio exploring how careful AI can support real people: authors, analysts, and service businesses that depend on trust and clarity.

Where we are now

We are in the early build phase, working toward our first working prototypes of Responder AI and AirScribe. A small set of early adopters will be invited to test core workflows before anything is made broadly available.

The focus is simple: practical tools that are fast, reliable, and honest about what they can and cannot do.

What we care about

  • Respecting human judgment and keeping humans in the loop.
  • Designing for privacy, security, and future compliance requirements from day one.
  • Building systems that stay understandable, even as they become more capable.

As Utuscribe grows, more details about the roadmap will be shared here ahead of larger announcements.
